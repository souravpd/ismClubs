About Us

The Official Filmography club of IIT (ISM)

We learn to make films in the club. Although group classes are definitely fun, individual training sessions give a chance to express emotions in full. If you are a beginning filmaker and want to get some confidence before participating in group classes – it's a great possibility for you. Pro filmaker with a level way above average looking to maintain their skill are welcome here too. Our experts can make a new movie per your request or come up with a unique movie specifically for you.

We make Screenplays for various skits What's the better way for a skit to succed? You don't need any previous acting experience to be the best writer at the club. Just a little confidence and practice – and with our help the results will come very soon.
